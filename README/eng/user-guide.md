# ReviewHub User Guide (Customer)

## How to Leave a Review

### Step 1: Receive SMS

You will receive an SMS from a company with a link to the review form:

```
Company asks for a review: https://review.reviewhub.ru/review/abc123
Unsubscribe: https://review.reviewhub.ru/optout/abc123
```

### Step 2: Open the Link

Tap the link in the SMS. A mobile review form (PWA) will open.

### Step 3: Rate and Write

1. **Give a rating** — from 1 to 5 stars
2. **Write a review** — minimum 10 characters, maximum 2000
3. Tap **"Submit"**

### Step 4: Result

#### If the review is positive:
- You will be offered to leave a review on Yandex Maps
- Tap the button — the company's Yandex Maps page will open
- Post your review on Yandex Maps (optional)

#### If the review is negative:
- You will see "Thank you for your feedback"
- You will receive a **promo code** for a discount on your next visit
- The company will receive your review and can improve their service

## Unsubscribe from SMS

If you don't want to receive SMS from this company:

1. Tap the "Unsubscribe" link in any SMS
2. You will see a confirmation: "You have been unsubscribed from SMS"
3. No more SMS from this company will be sent

## FAQ

### Link doesn't open
Make sure the full link was copied. Links are valid for 30 days.

### "Link expired"
The link was created more than 30 days ago and is no longer active.

### "Review already submitted"
A review has already been submitted via this link. Duplicate submissions are not allowed.

### Is my data safe?
Yes. Your phone number is encrypted. Data is stored on a server in Russia per 152-FZ requirements.
